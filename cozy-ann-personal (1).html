🌿 Recovery Exercises
💨
Box Breathing
4 min
Inhale 4 counts → hold 4 → exhale 4 → hold 4. Repeat 4 times. Activates the parasympathetic nervous system within 90 seconds. Use any time mental load hits 7+.
BEST FOR: Anxiety · Overwhelm · High Mental Load
✋
5-4-3-2-1 Grounding
2 min
Name 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste. Grounds you in the present moment instantly. Works for anxiety spirals and decision paralysis.
BEST FOR: Panic · Dissociation · Spiralling thoughts
☀️
Morning Sunlight
15 min
Step outside within 30 minutes of waking. No sunglasses. Even on cloudy days. Sets your circadian rhythm, boosts cortisol at the right time, and dramatically improves night sleep quality.
BEST FOR: Low energy · Poor sleep · Mood regulation
🚶
Phone-Free Walk
20 min
Walk slowly with no destination and no phone. Let your mind wander freely. Default mode network activity during unfocused walking is proven to restore cognitive function and unlock creative thinking.
BEST FOR: Mental fatigue · Creative blocks · Stuck thinking
💪
Progressive Muscle Relax
10 min
Tense each muscle group for 5 seconds, then release for 30. Start at your feet and work up to your head. Releases stored stress from the body. Best done lying down before sleep.
BEST FOR: Physical tension · Exhaustion · Pre-sleep
✍️
3 Good Things
5 min
Write 3 specific things that went well today and WHY they went well. Research by Martin Seligman shows this increases wellbeing measurably within 2 weeks of daily practice.
BEST FOR: Low motivation · Cynicism · Negative spirals
📵
Digital Sunset
Daily habit
No screens 60 minutes before sleep. Blue light suppresses melatonin production by up to 50%. Replace with reading, journaling, stretching, or a warm drink. Sleep quality visibly improves within a week.
BEST FOR: Poor sleep · Racing mind · Restlessness
💧
Cold Water Face Splash
30 sec
Splash cold water on your face 5-10 times. Triggers the mammalian dive reflex — instantly lowers heart rate and activates calm focus. The fastest nervous system reset available anywhere, anytime.
BEST FOR: Panic · Afternoon energy dip · Quick reset

Matthew Walker
The definitive guide to sleep science. Will permanently change how you think about rest.
The Nap Ministry
Tricia Hersey
Rest as resistance and radical self-care. Permission to stop without guilt.
💜 Emotional Burnout & Boundaries
Burnout
Emily & Amelia Nagoski
The science of what burnout actually is and the stress cycle you need to complete to heal.
Set Boundaries Find Peace
Nedra Tawwab
Practical boundary-setting. Essential if people-pleasing is draining your energy.
Laziness Does Not Exist
Devon Price
Reframes "laziness" as unmet needs. Gives permission to rest without shame.
🧠 Mental Burnout & Focus
Stolen Focus
Johann Hari
Why your attention is broken and who profits from it. Essential for the digital age.
Four Thousand Weeks
Oliver Burkeman
A radical reframe of time and productivity. Helps you stop trying to do everything.
Deep Work
Cal Newport
The case for focused work and how to build it. Pairs beautifully with your energy map.
🎯 Purpose & Motivation
Essentialism
Greg McKeown
The disciplined pursuit of less. Helps you identify what actually matters to you.
Dopamine Nation

Why rest feels hard and how to recalibrate your reward system. Deeply relevant.

Jenny Odell
Radical permission to resist the attention economy and simply be. Quietly life-changing.
